üí° Key Insights

  • Gender Progress: Female representation increased from 0% in early 1900s to approximately 12% in recent decades
  • Age Patterns: Literature laureates tend to be oldest (avg ~65 years), Physics laureates youngest (avg ~55 years)
  • Geographic Trends: United States dominates with most laureates, followed by United Kingdom and Germany
  • Category Distribution: Medicine and Physics have the highest number of laureates
  • Historical Growth: Number of laureates per year has steadily increased over time
